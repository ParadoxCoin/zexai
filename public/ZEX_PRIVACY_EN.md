# ZexAI Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 3, 2026
**Scope:** Global (Entire ZexAI Ecosystem)

At ZexAI, we deeply believe in **data privacy and security**, a cornerstone of the Web3 philosophy. Behind the content our users produce and the tools they use lies a transparent, non-tracking, ownership-based system.

By using the ZexAI platform ("application", "services", "we"), you accept the data processing methods outlined in this policy.

## 1. Data Collected (Minimum Data Principle)
ZexAI prefers a fully decentralized wallet infrastructure, unlike traditional Web2 platforms (passwords, email lists, secret databases).

- **Web3 Wallet Information:** When you log in to the ZexAI ecosystem (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.), we only receive your wallet address (`0x...`) that is publicly visible "On-Chain." Access to your wallet password, seed phrases, or assets in your wallet is never possible.
- **In-Platform Activities:** To improve your experience on the application, we temporarily cache anonymous activities within the ZexAI interface such as "Prompts" (AI text commands), generated "NFT images," or "Daily Streaks."
- **Telegram/Discord Information:** Your Telegram/Discord username may be processed in our database only if you participate in reward campaigns or airdrops, and only with your explicit permission. This information is never sold to third-party marketers.

## 2. Privacy of Generated Content (Art Works)
- Images or projects generated in **private mode** on ZexAI belong solely to that wallet owner. Unless you activate the public gallery sharing option, the prompts (templates) or works you create with AI are not shared with anyone.
- Smart contract logs (Minting, Purchase transactions, etc.) generated with your individual wallet address are **publicly available blockchain records** on EVM (Ethereum) based networks. ZexAI does not have the authority to hide these on-chain transactions.

## 3. Personal Data Collected for Robot Shipping (Founder's Edition)
To receive ZexAI "Founder's Edition" physical Humanoid Robots, the information you provide through our special order panel opening after presale:
- Physical Address (for delivery)
- Contact Information (for customs and shipping processes)

Is securely (encrypted) stored solely for the purpose of delivering your product through international logistics partners, and is permanently deleted from our database after product delivery is completed (except for financial audit processes).

## 4. Cookies and Web Analytics
The ZexAI platform may use standard web analytics tracking pixels / cookies (Google Analytics or similar) to anonymously segment visitor traffic, measure site performance, and make UI/UX improvements. You can disable your browser's cookie settings at any time.

## 5. Age Restriction
Our service is not intended for individuals under the age of 18 (or under the age of majority in your jurisdiction). Since the system cannot verify the user's identity due to the Web3 wallet infrastructure, all age and legal verification declarations are deemed to have been made by the user.

## 6. Data Deletion and "Right to Be Forgotten"
Your profile stored on ZexAI servers (including your private gallery and prompt history, if any) is immediately deleted upon request. Please note that token transfers, stake transactions, or minted NFT content created on the blockchain network are irreversible by nature of the network and cannot be deleted by ZexAI.

*For any questions about our privacy principles and data security, please contact authorized ZexAI personnel.*
